Resend has hired Ben Anderson as an Ops Generalist to build out vendor, finance, and compliance operations. Based in London, Ben brings experience from his previous role at Treecard where he developed operating infrastructure for startups. He was drawn to Resend's transparency and team culture, and advises aspiring operators to seek ownership and learn from experienced mentors.
